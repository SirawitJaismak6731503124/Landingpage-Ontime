Features

Exact time reminders

Set any time down to the minute. Ontime fires your notification at exactly the right moment, every time.

Weekly repeat

Choose exactly which days to repeat — Monday to Sunday. Weekdays, weekends, or any custom combination.

Unlimited routines

Morning run, vitamins, reading, meditation — add as many routines as you need with no limits.

Enable or disable

Pause any routine with a tap without losing it. Re-enable anytime — no need to recreate from scratch.

How it works

1

Create a routine

Give it a name — "Morning run", "Take vitamins", "Read before bed". Anything you want to remember.

2

Set a time and days

Pick the exact time and which days of the week to repeat. Weekdays, weekends, or fully custom.

3

Get notified on time

Ontime sends a notification at the exact scheduled time, every week, on the days you chose.

Privacy

Ontime stores all routines locally on your phone only. No account required. No internet connection needed. No data is ever collected or sent anywhere. Uninstall the app and everything is gone.
